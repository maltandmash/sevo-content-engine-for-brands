# Repurposing pack

> SEvO is about being everywhere the audience looks, not one page. After the article is done, the SEvO agent produces this pack so one piece of research feeds the third-party surfaces AI engines trust. It does not invent facts here either: everything traces back to the article and `approved-facts.md`.

Only build the surfaces that fit the client and sector. A regulated finance client is not going on TikTok with yield talk. Use judgement and the compliance map.

## Reddit answer (highest GEO value)

Reddit is one of the most-cited sources in AI answers. The job is to be genuinely useful, not to drop links.

- 90 percent help, 10 percent brand. If the brand cannot be mentioned without it reading as a pitch, leave it out.
- Title as a real question, not a headline: "What is the best X for a small team?"
- Lead with a TL;DR. Users and AI crawlers both value it.
- Write from first-hand experience where the client has it. No fabricated experience.
- Suggest two or three subreddits to target and note that trust is earned over time, not in one post.

## LinkedIn article or post

- Long-form, indexable, professional authority angle on the same topic.
- Keyword-rich headline and a strong first two lines.
- First-person point of view from the named author.

## YouTube brief

- Title with the primary query in the first 60 characters.
- Description: keyword-rich, the first two lines matter most.
- 8 to 12 tags, 2 to 3 hashtags.
- A high-contrast thumbnail concept.
- A hook for the first few seconds and an outline that holds watch time.

## TikTok or Reels brief

- Hook in the first 3 seconds.
- The algorithm reads captions, on-screen text and the spoken script, so put the target keywords in all three.
- A short shot list that earns retention.

## Instagram or Facebook post (now indexable)

Public professional accounts get indexed by Google, so a post is an evergreen SEO asset.

- Rename the image or video file to brand-product-keyword before upload.
- Put the keyword phrase in the first line of the caption.
- Write descriptive alt text for every post.
- Use a location tag where local search matters.

## Entity and measurement note

- Confirm the brand entity reads identically across these surfaces: name, what they do, key facts. Inconsistent entity data confuses the models.
- Remind the operator what to track for this piece: brand and domain share of voice, citation count and sentiment, plus the platform-native metric for each surface used.
